Paint Budget Calculator: Estimate Your Painting Costs Easily

Are you someone who has just bought their dream home or are trying to give a fresh look to their home and are utterly confused with the cost of painting? Then Voila! Asian Paints Paint Budget Calculator is all you need. Our paint budget calculator is used to ease the manual process that is required to measure paint and the amount of painting cost per square foot for your walls. With the calculator, you can get an estimate for your house painting cost in a few simple steps without any hassle of calling contractors and others.

How to Use the Paint Budget Calculator?

With our Asian Paints Paint Budget Calculator you can find the right estimates for your painting cost per square foot or house painting cost with just a few simple details of your home and the 4 simple steps given below -

Step 1: Choose the Type of the Project

The first step is selecting whether your project is for an interior or exterior painting job. The painting cost per sf changes based on this decision, as interior and exterior paints have different pre-painting requirements, painting processes, and other requirements.

Step 2: Select Interior or Exterior Paint

Next, decide whether you need interior paint or exterior paint. The painting price per square foot for exterior jobs is usually higher because exterior paints the painting price per square foot can change depending on the advanced exterior paints you use for the wall to withstand harsh weather, while interior paints can be different considering the paint formulations focus more on appearance and indoor wear resistance.

Step 3: Input Your Room Dimensions

Enter the dimensions of the room or area you plan to paint. The total painting price per square foot will depend on the size of the space, as larger rooms will require more paint and labour, impacting your overall budget.
